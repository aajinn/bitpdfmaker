Extract & Embed Notes Into PDF

BitPDFMaker is your one-stop tool to extract key points, summaries, and micro notes from PDF documents and place them smartly into small square blocks inside the same PDF. Perfect for quick revision or compressed overviews.

Why Use BitPDFMaker?

  • Convert long documents into bite-sized note PDFs
  • Insert square-shaped notes directly into your PDF
  • Quick download for offline usage
  • Completely private - nothing is stored

How It Works

  1. Upload your PDF document using the tool above.
  2. Our tool scans and extracts key content such as headings, bullet points, and definitions.
  3. Notes are automatically compressed into small readable square boxes.
  4. You download a new PDF with those embedded blocks ready for printing or revision.
